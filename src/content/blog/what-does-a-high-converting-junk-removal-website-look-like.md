## High-Converting Website Strategy for Junk Removal Business

The primary goal of a high-converting junk removal website is to turn visitors into booked customers by guiding them to take action like calling for a free estimate, scheduling a pickup online, or requesting a quote.

Every element of your website should work toward converting your audience into paying customers and trust you to handle their junk removal needs.

## Clear CTA (Call to Action)

We want a clear CTA that guides users towards taking the desired action. In this case, making a call.

## Page Speed

We optimize the website's page loading speed as it's one of the key factors that Google use to evaluate user experience.

No one wants a slow loading website. Generally, anything over 3 seconds is considered too long.

One way we optimize the website's loading speed is to reduce image file size and convert images to .webp.
