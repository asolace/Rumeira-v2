## How we strategize on redesigning a website that sets up the users to take the desired actions

Every design decision's goal is to build trust and ultimately, to turn visitors into customers by guiding them to take action like calling or scheduling a discovery call.

## Color Palette & Brand Theme

Our process begins with your logo and brand identity, enhancing them through a color system designed to evoke trust and emotion.

We also offer recommendations for enhancing and refining the logo. In this case, the client chose to keep their existing logo.

## Researching Existing Content & Online Presence

We do a deep-dive in our research on ALL (We mean all!) of the brand’s current digital footprint to understand the voice, tone, and perception that already exists.

- Website copy
- Social media accounts
- The owners social media accounts
- and other relevant media mentions

## Competitor Research

We analyze industry competitors to identify trends, user expectations, and opportunities for differentiation.

Using research tools such as SEMrush, Ahrefs, Search Atlas, and simple Google searches, we gather insights that guide our strategy.

This ensures the new design not only matches but outperforms others in usability, clarity, and conversion strategy.

## Keyword Research

We compile a list of keywords that best represent the business, its services, and the audience’s search intent.
‍

**Primary Keyword**
The home page focuses on high-impact, broad keywords that define the brand and attract primary traffic.

**High-impact, Low Effort Keywords**
When the client transitions into a long-term SEO strategy, we expand into service-specific and location-based keywords to strengthen overall visibility and capture more qualified leads.

## Website Layout | Mapping the User Journey

This is where design meets psychology.

**Building Trust & Taking Action**
Each layout is crafted to guide users from awareness to decision.
We align messaging, visuals, and CTAs to build user trust.

Every element is designed to encourage the next step, whether it’s submitting a claim, booking a consultation, or calling directly.

## Content Transition & Implementation

We take existing content from the old website and combine it with insights gathered from our brand and competitor research.

## Testimonials & Proof

Trust builds conversions. We incorporate verified client testimonials and case results throughout key touch points to strengthen credibility and influence user action.
